Who I Am

A Creative Mind
Rooted in Science

I am Cornell Osetale Isikhuemen — a multidisciplinary creative and academic based in Nigeria. My work sits at the intersection of visual storytelling, scientific inquiry, and emerging technology.

As a photographer, I have collaborated with studios including Reff Jey, Pleroma Studios, Lily Studios, and Radi8 Imagery, bringing visual narratives to life across diverse contexts. My graphic design work complements this creative practice.

Academically, I am a published researcher in environmental biology and a proud fellow of the Learn2Earn Cohort 2 programme — actively developing skills in software development to add a new dimension to everything I do.
